1. Alin ang may wastong gamit ng gitling?

B. Mag-aaral ako

C. Pag-asa ng bayan

D. Bawat-isa

A. Dancing needs a partner

B. Both parties are responsible for a situation

C. Two is better than one

D. Tango is for couples only

Explanation: Idiomatic expression meaning both people involved are responsible.

3. Which sentence is grammatically correct?
